This project is a conversational website between the English speaking and non English speaking communities of India. It features three questions with audio based answers. The English speakers belong to the urban youth of India or the digital generation. This group of people are growing more distant from their Indian roots because they interact and engage with their language less and less each day. A majority of the speakers do not know how to read and write their mother tongues. On the contrary, the older generation communicates in a regional Indian language to their social groups which keeps them immersed and connected to their culture.
